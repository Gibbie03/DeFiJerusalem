## Cold Email Best Practices

### Subject Line Tips:
✓ Keep it under 50 characters  
✓ Personalize with protocol name  
✓ Avoid spam words ("FREE", "ACT NOW")  
✓ Use numbers when relevant  

### Email Body:
✓ Keep it under 150 words  
✓ One clear call-to-action  
✓ Personalize with specific data  
✓ Use bullet points for scannability  
✓ Include social proof  

### Timing:
✓ Send Tuesday-Thursday, 9-11 AM (recipient's timezone)  
✓ Follow up after 3-5 days  
✓ Max 2-3 follow-ups  
✓ Space follow-ups 5-7 days apart  

### What NOT to Do:
✗ Generic "Dear Sir/Madam"  
✗ Long paragraphs  
✗ Multiple CTAs  
✗ Attach large files  
✗ All caps or excessive exclamation marks

- Don't use misleading subject lines
