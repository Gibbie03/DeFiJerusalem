## 📝 Writing Tips

### Tone & Style
- **Educational but accessible** - Explain technical concepts simply
- **Trustworthy** - Cite sources, use real examples
- **Action-oriented** - Give readers clear next steps
- **No hype** - Be honest about risks

### Content Guidelines
- **Word count**: 1,800-3,000 words (longer = better for SEO)
- **Structure**: Use H2/H3 headings for scannability
- **Examples**: Include 2-3 real-world case studies per post
- **CTAs**: Link to JERUSALEM scanner throughout
- **Updates**: Refresh quarterly with new scams

### SEO Best Practices
- **Target keyword** in title, first 100 words, H2 headings
- **Internal links**: Link to 5-10 other blog posts
- **External links**: Link to 3-5 authoritative sources
- **Images**: Include 5-10 images with descriptive alt text
- **Meta description**: 150-160 characters, include keyword
