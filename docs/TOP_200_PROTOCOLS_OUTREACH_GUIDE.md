## How to Find Email Addresses

Since email addresses are not publicly available in DeFiLlama data, here are proven strategies:

### Method 1: Website Contact Pages
Visit each protocol's website and look for:
- `/contact`
- `/about`
- `/team`
- Footer sections with "Contact Us"

**Common email patterns**:
- `partnerships@[domain].com`
- `business@[domain].com`
- `contact@[domain].com`
- `bd@[domain].com` (Business Development)
- `hello@[domain].com`

### Method 2: Twitter/X Direct Messages
- Follow their Twitter account
- Send a professional DM introducing JERUSALEM
- Many protocols prefer Twitter for initial contact

### Method 3: GitHub
- Check the GitHub organization members
- Look for `SECURITY.md` or `CONTRIBUTING.md` files
- Contact emails are often listed there

### Method 4: LinkedIn
Search for:
- "[Protocol Name] Business Development"
- "[Protocol Name] Partnerships"
- "[Protocol Name] Marketing Director"

### Method 5: Email Finder Tools
Use these tools (paid/freemium):
- **Hunter.io**: Find emails by domain
- **Apollo.io**: B2B contact database
- **RocketReach**: Professional contact finder
- **Clearbit Connect**: Chrome extension for email lookup
