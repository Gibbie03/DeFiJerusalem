## 4. Valuation Methodologies

### Methodology 1: Venture Capital Method

**Approach:** Estimate future exit valuation and discount to present value.

**Assumptions:**
- **Exit Timeframe:** 5 years (2030)
- **Exit Multiple:** 10x ARR (conservative for crypto security SaaS)
- **Target ARR at Exit:** $25M (modest market share capture)
- **Required VC Return:** 10x (typical for early-stage crypto/tech)

**Calculation:**
```
Exit Valuation = $25M ARR × 10x = $250M
Present Value = $250M / 10x return = $25M

Adjust for pre-revenue risk (60% discount) = $25M × 0.40 = $10M
```

**VC Method Result: $10M**

## 7. Risk Factors

### Market Risks

#### 1. Crypto Market Volatility (-15% to -30% valuation impact)
- **Risk:** Prolonged bear market reduces protocol marketing budgets
- **Likelihood:** Medium (cyclical nature of crypto markets)
- **Mitigation:** Focus on security-first positioning (always needed) vs. pure marketing
- **Scenario:** If crypto TVL drops 50%, expect 30% reduction in sponsor demand

#### 2. Competitive Pressure (-10% to -20% impact)
- **Risk:** CertiK or GoPlus launch competing sponsorship platforms
- **Likelihood:** Low-Medium (not core to their audit/API business models)
- **Mitigation:** Network effects from community reporting, content moat, brand loyalty
- **Scenario:** New competitor could fragment market, reducing pricing power

#### 3. Regulatory Uncertainty (-20% to -40% impact)
- **Risk:** DeFi regulation bans/restricts protocol operations in key markets
- **Likelihood:** Medium (EU MiCA implementation ongoing, US unclear)
- **Mitigation:** Pivot to compliance-focused offerings, expand to non-US markets
- **Scenario:** US DeFi crackdown could reduce TAM by 35-40%

### Operational Risks

#### 4. Solo Founder Dependency (-30% to -50% impact)
- **Risk:** Single point of failure for development, sales, operations
- **Likelihood:** High (current structure)
- **Mitigation:** Hire technical co-founder, build team with seed funding
- **Scenario:** Founder burnout or departure would severely impact execution

#### 5. Revenue Conversion Uncertainty (-20% to -35% impact)

- **Likelihood:** Medium (untested go-to-market strategy)
- **Mitigation:** A/B test outreach messaging, offer trials, build case studies early

- **Likelihood:** Low-Medium (PostgreSQL + Neon scales well, but costs increase)
- **Mitigation:** Optimize database queries, implement caching, use CDN

### Technology Risks

#### 8. Data Quality Issues (-10% to -20% impact)

- **Likelihood:** Medium (38+ threat categories create complexity)
- **Mitigation:** Manual review queue, whitelist system, community feedback loops

### Financial Risks

#### 9. Burn Rate Management (-10% to -25% impact)
